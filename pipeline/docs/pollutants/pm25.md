## 4. NAAQS

- **Annual mean: 9.0 µg/m³** (revised Feb 2024, down from 12.0)
- **24-hr 98th percentile: 35 µg/m³**

⚠ **All three Coastal Bend PM2.5 sites EXCEED the new 9.0 µg/m³ annual
NAAQS in 2024.** Two of three also exceed in 2023. **This is the headline
finding for the Coastal Bend.**

## 9. ML modeling considerations

Three strategies for method transitions:

- **A. Method-209-only** — 3 sites × 2018+ (Kingsville) / 2018+ (Dona
  Park) / 2022+ (CC Holly). Clean but small.
- **B. Full dataset with method as categorical covariate** — more data,
  model has to learn method offset.
- **C. Two-era models** (TEOM 2015-2021 vs BAM 2018-2025) with physical
  consistency comparison.
